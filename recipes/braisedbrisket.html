Braised Corn Beef Brisket

Ingredients

  • 1 5 pound flat-cut corned beef brisket
  • 1 tbsp browning sauce
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 2 tbsp water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C)
  2. Discard flavoring packet of corned beef. Brush brisket with browing sauce.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Brown on both sides, 5 to 8 mins per side.
  4. Place brisket on a rack set in a roasting pan. Scatter onion and garlic over the brisket and add water. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  5. Roast in a preheat oven until the meat is tender, for about six hours.
